Chen Liu is a Golomb Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Purdue University under the supervision of Prof. Xiangxiong Zhang. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computational and Applied Mathematics at Rice University. My advisor is Prof. Beatrice Riviere. Click here for my detailed Curriculum Vitae.

My research involves the study of numerical techniques for partial differential equations. A few key topics are listed below.

  • Discontinuous Galerkin methods
  • Incompressible Navier-Stokes
  • Pore-scale multi-phase flow
  • Hyperbolic conservation laws
  • Nonsmooth convex optimization
